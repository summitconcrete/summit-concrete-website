Our Installation Process in Vail

Most epoxy garage floor projects in Vail are completed in one to two days. Here's what the process looks like:

1

Diamond Grinding & Slab Prep

We mechanically profile the concrete with commercial diamond grinders — opening the pores for proper adhesion. This step is critical in Vail's mountain climate. Acid etching (used in DIY kits) doesn't cut it at altitude.

2

Moisture-Blocking Epoxy Base Coat

We apply a moisture-vapor-blocking epoxy primer and base coat — important for Vail slabs where freeze-thaw ground moisture can cause delamination in lesser products. Your chosen flake blend gets broadcast while the base is still wet.

3

Polyaspartic Topcoat

After scraping and vacuuming excess flakes, we apply a commercial polyaspartic or polyurethane topcoat. UV stable and chemical resistant — it won't yellow or chalk from Vail's intense high-altitude UV exposure.

4

Drive-In Ready in 48–72 Hours

Light foot traffic in 24 hours. Park your vehicles in 48–72 hours. Your Vail garage is ready for another ski season, backed by a lifetime warranty.

Why DIY Epoxy Fails in Vail

Vail sits at over 8,000 feet. That elevation creates conditions that expose weaknesses in budget epoxy products fast:

  • Road salt and de-icer — Vail's streets and I-70 are heavily treated all winter. That salt gets tracked into garages on tires and ski boots and eats through thin topcoats quickly.
  • Freeze-thaw movement — Concrete in Vail expands and contracts more dramatically than at low elevation. Coatings without proper prep and the right chemistry delaminate from beneath.
  • High UV at altitude — UV intensity at 8,000+ ft is significantly higher than at sea level. DIY topcoats yellow and chalk. Our polyaspartic topcoats are UV stable and rated for it.
  • Moisture vapor transmission — Mountain ground moisture migrates through concrete slabs and blisters coatings that don't use a proper vapor-blocking primer.

Every system we install in Vail uses commercial-grade materials selected specifically for this climate — not off-the-shelf kits. Our lifetime warranty reflects that confidence.
