Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Summit Concrete Finishes ("Summit Concrete Finishes," "we," "us," or "our") provides epoxy garage floor coating and concrete finishing services in Summit County and the surrounding mountain communities of Colorado. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website at www.summitconcretefinishes.com (the "Site"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Site or submitting a request through it, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

Information you give us. When you request a free estimate or otherwise contact us, we collect the details you choose to provide through our form, which may include:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your phone number and email address
  • Your town or service location
  • Your project timeline and a description of the work you'd like done
  • How you heard about us

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our hosting provider automatically records standard technical information when you visit — such as your IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, and the pages you view. This information is used to keep the Site secure and running reliably.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your estimate request and contact you about your project
  • Schedule and provide our services
  • Answer your questions and provide customer support
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the Site
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not use it for unrelated advertising.

How We Share Information

We share your information only as needed to operate our business:

  • Service providers. Our contact form is processed by Formspree, which delivers your submission to us by email. Our Site is hosted by Vercel. These providers process information on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal reasons. We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our customers, our business, or others.

Third-Party Services

The Site relies on a small number of trusted third parties:

  • Formspree — handles delivery of contact-form submissions. See Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Cookies

We do not use advertising or analytics cookies to track you across websites. Our third-party providers may set limited cookies that are necessary to deliver and secure the Site. You can control or block cookies through your browser settings; doing so will not prevent you from contacting us.

Data Retention

We keep the information you submit for as long as needed to respond to your request, provide our services, and meet recordkeeping or legal requirements. When it is no longer needed, we delete it or remove information that identifies you.

Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, to request a correction, or to ask us to delete it. If you've asked us to follow up and change your mind, just let us know and we'll stop contacting you. To make any of these requests, reach us using the contact details below.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for a general, adult audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Effective date" above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
