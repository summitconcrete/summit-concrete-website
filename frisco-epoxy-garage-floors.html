Why Cheap Epoxy Fails in Frisco

Frisco's location adds a few extra pressure points beyond the usual mountain-climate challenges:

  • Lake-effect moisture — Being right on Lake Dillon means more ambient humidity and ground moisture than towns set back from the water. Coatings without a proper vapor-blocking base coat blister and lift.
  • Interstate road salt — Frisco sits at a major I-70 interchange, and that highway gets salted hard all winter. It gets tracked into garages fast.
  • Freeze-thaw movement — Concrete at 9,000+ feet expands and contracts more than lower-elevation slabs. Coatings without proper mechanical prep delaminate from beneath.
  • High-altitude UV — Budget epoxy topcoats yellow and chalk in Frisco's intense mountain sun. Our polyaspartic topcoats are UV-stable and rated for it.

Every system we install in Frisco uses commercial-grade materials chosen for this exact combination of conditions — not an off-the-shelf kit.

Caring for Your Floor Through a Frisco Winter

A properly installed floor doesn't need much upkeep, but a few habits make a real difference in how it looks after several Frisco winters:

  • Rinse off standing salt brine — A quick hose-down or wet-mop after a heavy salt week keeps de-icer residue from sitting on the surface for weeks at a time. It's cosmetic, not structural, but it keeps the floor looking new.
  • Use a squeegee for big snowmelt days — On days when several inches of snow melt off a vehicle at once, pushing the water toward the floor drain or garage door threshold prevents it from pooling in one spot for hours.
  • Don't worry about ice-melt products — Unlike bare concrete, a fully cured polyaspartic topcoat isn't damaged by common calcium chloride or magnesium chloride ice-melt products tracked in on boots.
  • Watch for felt or rubber floor mats — Mats that trap moisture underneath for weeks can occasionally cause slight discoloration on any coated floor. Lift and dry them out periodically during heavy snow months.

None of this is required to keep the lifetime warranty valid — it's simply what keeps a Frisco garage floor looking as good in year five as it did on installation day.
